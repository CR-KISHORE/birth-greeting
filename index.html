A little letter, from me to you

Dear Sri Varshini,

This birthday is different. For four beautiful years, I stood beside you as your best friend — laughing with you, fighting for you, keeping your secrets, and quietly falling for you a little more every single day. Every year I wished you "Happy Birthday, my best friend." This year, for the first time, I get to say — Happy Birthday, my love.

Nothing about you changed, and yet everything changed. The same smile I knew for years now makes my heart skip. The same voice that once comforted me as a friend now feels like home. They say the best love stories begin with friendship — ours began with the deepest one I've ever known.

And this is only the beginning. Somewhere not far from today — a year, maybe a little more — I'll stand in front of you, in front of everyone, and ask you to be mine forever. Until that ring finds your finger, let my words hold you: you are already my forever. You were my best friend first, you are my love now, and soon you'll be my fiancée — but in my heart, you've always been my whole world.

So make a big wish today, Varshini. And know that while you're wishing, I already got mine — four years ago, the day you walked into my life.

— Your best friend, forever your lover ♡
